    About GeoTrendViz

    GeoTrendViz is a powerful tool for visualizing Twitter trends by location. Explore trending topics, analyze tweet volumes, and discover what's happening around the world.

    Features:

    • Location-based trend visualization
    • Multi-language support
    • Interactive word cloud
    • Real-time tweet data
    • Dark/Light mode
